Chicken Salad

Description

This delicious chicken salad is the perfect post-workout meal to get your vitamins and protein in.

Calories Carbs Fat Protein
164 5g 9g 15g

Ingredients

  • Cooked and seasoned chicken
  • Cucumbers
  • Carrots
  • Avocados
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetables and chicken into bite size pieces
  2. Mix well
  3. Top it off with your favourite dressing
