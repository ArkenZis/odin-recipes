Oatmeal Smoothie

Description

This healthy Oatmeal Smoothie is the perfect go to smoothie to start of your day

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Honey
  • Chocolate Powder
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Add appropriate oats to a blender
  2. Add a cup of milk
  3. Add a spoon of honey
  4. Add a spoon of chocolate powder
  5. Add two spoons of peanut butter
  6. Blend the mixture and it's ready to be served
